I'm a recent graduate from The University of Washington Bothell's graduate certificate program in software engineering. I've spent my summer traveling a bit and visiting family and I'm now looking forward to finding an entry level position as a junior software developer. Originally, I set out to work in the medical field and attained a bachelor's in Biological Sciences from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. After acquiring my undergraduate degree, I realized that my passion for the medical field never really developed and I just enjoyed the sciences. It took a year of self-reflection and I decided to pursue a career in the one thing I've always enjoyed... computers! I decided to move myself to Seattle where there are numerous opportunities in hopes that I can grow my career as a software developer. The influences on my coding and style come from my love of minimalism. I believe a strong impact can be made without a convoluted interface.
